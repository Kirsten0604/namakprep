About Na Maka K-Prep

Na Maka Kindergarten Prep School was established in 2015 to give children a head start in education. We at Na Maka Kindergarten Prep School are dedicated to get every child prepared to enter kindergarten with the necessary skills to excel. We provide a well-rounded education through Language (Reading and Writing), Math exploration, inquiry, social and cognitive skills, creative movement, music, and fine arts.
