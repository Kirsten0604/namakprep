Our Mission

Na Maka Kindergarten Prep School Is dedicated to providing a high quality / enrichment educational opportunity for children ages 2 - 5.

We instill a lifetime love of learning and development of value for themselves, their family, and our community.

We provide a well-rounded education through language (reading and writing), math exploration, inquiry, social and cognitive skills, creative movement, music, and fine arts.
